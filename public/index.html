API Documentation

Below is a list of available API endpoints for this project.

# Method URL Description
1 GET /phones/q1 Fetch the first 10 phones sorted by ID in ascending order.
2 GET /phones/q2 Fetch the top 5 most expensive phones sorted by price in descending order.
3 GET /phones/q3 Fetch 3 phones with the highest ratings, skipping the first 3 records.
4 GET /phones/q4 Fetch details of the phone with the name "Mi 10".
5 GET /phones/q5 Fetch phones that contain "Adreno" in their GPU specification.
6 GET /phones/q9 Get the total count of phones in the collection.
7 GET /phones/q10 Get the count of phones that use "Qualcomm" CPUs.
8 GET /phones/q11 Fetch 4 phones with a price between 1300 and 2000, sorted by price in ascending order.
9 GET /phones/q12 Fetch phones with total scores of 86, 90, or 79.
10 GET /phones/q13 Fetch the name and total score of the first 10 phones, sorted by company ID.
11 GET /phones/q14 Fetch phones where the battery score is 76 or the company ID is 2, sorted by price in descending order.
12 GET /phones/q15 Fetch phones where the battery score is 76 and the company ID is 4.
13 GET /phones/q16 Get the count of phones grouped by company ID.
14 GET /phones/q17 Get the average price of phones grouped by company ID.
15 GET /phones/q18 Get the minimum price of phones grouped by company ID.
16 GET /phones/q19 Fetch all phones with company name populated.
17 GET /phones/q20 Fetch the top 5 most expensive phones with company country populated.
18 GET /companies Fetch all companies.
